Lasagne Al Forno

Baked lasagne with rich tomato and cheese layers

Description

Lasagne Al Forno is a classic Italian dish made by layering rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets. It’s baked to perfection with a golden, bubbly cheese topping, making it a comforting and hearty meal for family and friends.

This recipe combines savory ground beef, aromatic herbs, and a luscious tomato sauce, balanced with creamy layers of béchamel and melted cheese. Perfect for special occasions or cozy dinners!

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagne Sheets
  • 2 table spoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finly chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g beef mince
  • 2x 400g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp tomatoe puree
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • salt and papper to taste
  • 500ml bechemel sauce
  • 200g grated mozarella cheese
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large frying pan on a medium heat
  3. Add in the minced beef and cook until browned all over
  4. Stir in the tinned chopped tomatoes, tomato puree, oregano, salt and papper. Simmer for 20 mins
  5. In a baking dish, spread a think layer of meat sauce
  6. Layer lasagne sheets over the sauce
  7. Add a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of bechemel sauce, then sprinkle with mozarella
  8. Repeat layers, finishing with bechemel sauce and Parmesan cheese on top
  9. Put in the middle of the oven and cook for about 45 minutes: or until golden brown on top, bubbling around the edges and the pasta is soft.
  10. Let rest for 10 mins before serving, and enjoy!
